What is it?

The Conference and Events Branch is in charge of hosting educational outreach programs that help high school students gain exposure to an entrepreneurship mindset and skillset, and network with other business-oriented students and established business executives of the industry. Through conferences hosted by the Conference and Events Branch, young entrepreneurs will have the opportunity to meet with top notch business executives speakers as well as create lasting connections with other students interested or passionate about business. This branch is sponsored by McKinsey & Co, Draper University, Tivix, Y Combinator, and SiliconVal.ly. As with the Marketing Agency, a cut of the profits generated by the Conference and Events Branch from the conferences will be allocated to the Incubator sector of Foundation Ventures.
